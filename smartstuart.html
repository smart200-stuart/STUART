COOKING EGGS

Cooking is fun.

INGREDIENTS

  • EGGS
  • ONIONS
  • TOMATOES
  • SALT
  • BUTTER
  • PEPPER
  • KETCHUP

RECIPE

  1. Break the eggs and empty them into a bowl.
  2. Add a pinch of salt,pepper, and cut onions then stir for 20s.
  3. Put a spoonful of butter into the fryingpan and melt it.
  4. Put the eggs and ry until it becomes a crunch.
  5. Put the crunc on a plate and apply ketchp.
  6. Take a seat and treat yourself to a fried egg.
